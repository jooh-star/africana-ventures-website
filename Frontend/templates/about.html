Pioneering Agricultural Innovation

Africana Ventures Limited is an innovative agritech company based in Tanzania, committed to transforming agriculture through technology, sustainability, and farmer empowerment.

We focus on building end-to-end agricultural solutions that connect smallholder farmers to reliable markets, reduce post-harvest losses, and promote environmentally responsible practices. Our technology-driven approach includes both online platforms—such as mobile apps and websites—and offline access through USSD systems, ensuring that farmers across all regions, regardless of connectivity or device type, can benefit from our services.

Our Mission

We aim at empowering smallholder farmers by providing innovative, technology-driven solutions that connect them to markets, reduce post-harvest losses, and promote climate-smart agriculture. We aim to enhance the agricultural value chain, foster economic growth, and improve food security across Tanzania and Africa.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading technology company groups in Africa, creating and building sustainable businesses that deliver quality and affordable services and products to key social challenges while driving economic prosperity, job creation, and good returns to our shareholders, stakeholders, and partners.

BUSINESS STRATEGY

Our Multi-Faceted Business Model

Africana Ventures operates with a comprehensive business model designed to address the diverse needs of the agricultural sector. By integrating technology, logistics, and value-added services, we create an ecosystem that connects farmers, producers, wholesalers, retailers, and consumers.

Marketplace Model

Our marketplace platform allows agricultural producers to list and sell their products to a broader audience of buyers. We act as an intermediary between producers and consumers, facilitating efficient exchanges and generating revenue through transaction commissions.

Our Impact & Milestones

2015: Founding & Initial Pilot

Established Africana Ventures with a pilot program empowering 50 smallholder farmers in rural Kenya, focusing on sustainable practices.

2018: Market Linkage Expansion

Successfully connected over 1,000 farmers to urban markets, increasing their income by an average of 30% through fair trade practices.

2020: Regional Growth & Tech Integration

Expanded operations into Uganda and Tanzania, reaching over 2,000 new farmers and integrating mobile technology for agricultural extension services.

2022: Innovation & Community Resilience

Recognized for innovative climate-smart agriculture programs, building resilience against climate change impacts in vulnerable communities.

2024: Strategic Partnerships & Scaling Impact

Secured significant impact investment and forged strategic partnerships to scale our Agri-Marketplace platform, reaching 5,000+ farmers.
